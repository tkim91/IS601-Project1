AAA Testing

Arrange, Act, and Assert Pattern

The AAA or Arrange-Act-Assert pattern is an industry standard for testing. This pattern suggests that test methods should be divided into three sections, arrange, act, and assert, and each is only responsible for the part that they are named after.
